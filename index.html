About Me

Hello Friend,

For close to two decades, I've had the privilege of working alongside visionary leaders - from front-line staff to CEOs of major organizations - helping them harness technology to transform their businesses and achieve breakthrough results.

Recently, I led the delivery of AI-powered solutions that detected over $105 million worth of fraud in their first year, and spearheaded the largest bank business book migration in Canadian history as part of RBC's $13.5 billion HSBC acquisition. These aren't just projects to me - they're opportunities to create lasting impact.

Whether you're navigating digital transformation, scaling your business, or facing complex strategic challenges, I believe every organization has untapped potential waiting to be unlocked. My role is to help you see those possibilities and turn them into reality.

To your success,

Bod Idris

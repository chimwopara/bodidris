My Journey

Two decades of innovation, leadership, and transformation

2004-2008

Building the Foundation

Started my journey at CIBC as an Analyst, diving deep into records management and retention. These early years taught me the critical importance of data integrity and process optimization in financial services.

2008-2016

Mastering Business Intelligence

Transitioned into business analysis and intelligence at Alberta Blue Cross, then expanded into consulting. Led major initiatives for City of Edmonton, including COBIT 5 framework implementation and enterprise-wide transformations.

2016-2022

Strategic Consulting Excellence

Established myself as a premier consultant across multiple industries - from Government of Alberta's digital transformation to Edmonton Police Services' data analytics strategy. Built deep expertise in business-IT alignment and strategic roadmap development.

2022-Present

AI-Powered Transformation

Leading breakthrough AI initiatives that detected over $105 million in fraud. Spearheaded RBC's historic $13.5 billion HSBC acquisition - the largest bank migration in Canadian history. Teaching the next generation at NAIT while continuing to push the boundaries of what's possible.
